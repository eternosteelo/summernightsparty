ABOUT

Hola Mi Gente, this summer we are running it back with another great event from Eterno Steelo. We are celebrating our 10th anniversary by throwing our epic Summer Nights at Alamo Beer Company. Without all of you, our Eterno Steelo Familia, none of this would be possible so we want you to come celebrate this amazing achievement with us. Summer Nights with Eternosteelo takes place Saturday July 15th from 5pm - midnight.

Our events are the talk of the town with a focus on celebrating our culture and showcasing local talent. From the city's most talented muralists to our street photographers and videographers, we bring the best of the city to our curated events. This will be an unforgettable experience!

Come out and enjoy a FREE family friendly event with live music, djs, lightshow, local vendors, food vendors, live painting, photo opps, and show cars on display. Don't forget ice cold beer brewed fresh at Alamo Beer Company. We will have LIMITED EDITION event beer mugs for $20 which will get you $3 refills all evening!

Alamo Beer Company

5pm - 12am

Presented by Eterno Steelo clothing brand, established in 2013, by David Espinoza aka Davy. Davy has built a loyal following from years of networking through the brand and curating events that bring together the elements of the barrio/hispanic culture. Our brand represents familia, street Culture & the hustle of our people, which is highlighted through our apparel and events.
