LOWRIDERS

Thank you for your interest in the 2023 Summer Nights Party. This application is for interested individuals with lowriders. If selected, you will have a reserved parking space for this event to showcase your vehicle. This is FREE.

Space is limited and lowrider applications are reviewed on a first-come/first serve basis. Due to limited parking and safety concerns, limited spots are available. Signing up does not guarantee you will be selected. If you are selected, you will receive a confirmation email.

Please review the following guidelines:

  1. The vehicle must be parked in the assigned space
  2. The parking pass cannot be sold or transferred
  3. Owners are responsible for cleaning up their area
  4. Owners are responsible for the safety and security of their vehicle
  5. Any damages or theft that may occur to the vehicle or its contents during the event are the sole responsibility of the vehicle owner/representative.
